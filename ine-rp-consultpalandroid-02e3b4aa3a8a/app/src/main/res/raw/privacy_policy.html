Consult Pal APP Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Consult Pal ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by PsychComms. The Application is designed to supplement the medical and healthcare consultation experience through providing a safe and secure information sharing platform.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download and register the Application. Registration with us is optional. However, please keep in mind that you may not be able to use some of the features offered by the Application unless you register with us.

When you register with us and use the Application, you generally provide (a) your name, date of birth, and email address/ telephone number; (b) the name of your GP surgery or healthcare professional place of practice (c) information you provide us should you contact us for help (d) information you enter into our system when using the Application, such as your physical or mental health difficulties.

We may also use the information you provided us to contact your from time to time to provide you with important information, or required notices such as a change to this policy or the End User License Agreement.

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help us improve the Application and our service. We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a ‘witness summons’, or similar legal process;
  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.
  • if PsychComms is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of this information, as well as any choices you may have regarding this information.
  • to advertisers and third party advertising networks and analytics companies as described in the section below

Automatic Data Collection and Advertising

We may work with analytics companies to help us understand how the Application is being used, such as the frequency and duration of usage. We do not work with third party advertising networks. To protect the anonymity of this information, encryption technology is used to help ensure that you cannot be identified personally. Non-precise location information (e.g., your postcode), may be used to help analyze for improved service quality.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or tablet, via the mobile application marketplace or network. You can also request to opt-out via email, at info@consultpalapp.com

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. The information you share will be available for the GP or healthcare professional to insert on your Patient Record (electronic or paper-based). We will retain Automatically Collected information for up to 24 months and thereafter may store it in aggregate. If you’d like us to delete User Provided Data that you have provided via the Application, please contact us at info@consultpalapp.com and we will respond in a reasonable timeframe. Please note that some or all of the User Provided Data may be required in order for the Application to function properly.

Age of Consent

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 16. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at info@consultpalapp.com. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time. The app is to be used by adults 16 and over, in accordance with the legal age of NHS registry, however may be used on behalf of users under this age or users considered vulnerable or lacking in capacity via parent or legal guardian.

Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain. For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our Application. Please be aware that, although we endeavor provide reasonable security for information we process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.
